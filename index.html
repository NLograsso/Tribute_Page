My Twin Peaks Experience

It's not very often you get a chance to watch every single episode of your favorite show and actually visit where it all took place. From the falls to the famous RR Diner, this was a Twin Peaks fan dream come true. Imagining David Lynch walking through the woods of Twin Peaks, I can see why he was so fascinated with this place. It's just so separated from the rest of the world. Nature runs wild and the few that live there all live together like one large family. And yes, the coffee was "damn" fine.

Twin Peaks Checklist:

* Twin Peaks High School
* The Roadhouse
* The Falls
* RR Diner
* The Forgotten Train Tracks
* The Old Northern Hotel
